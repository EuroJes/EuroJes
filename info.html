About the European Jesuit Spirituality Centres

The Jesuit Conference of European Provincials (JCEP) unites the Provinces that govern the Jesuit presence in Europe, the Near East, Northern Africa and Central Asia. The Spirituality Centres are one element of the Jesuit presence in this region.
