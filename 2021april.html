European Jesuit Spirituality Centres Meeting - April 2021

Video of Thursday 8 April 2021 Meeting

Barton kindly agreed that his presentation could be recorded and we are happy to make it available to you who attended the meeting as long as it is not circulated more widely.

  • Part 1: What is Ignatius’s understanding of the greater glory of God. (Video)
    • What was the prevailing spirituality of the 16th century?
    • His spirituality shifts in Manresa from ascetical to apostolic.
    • This confirmed by his illumination at the river Cardoner.
    • At the University of Paris, he learns to give it a name: the more universal good.
    • This becomes the official charism of the new Jesuit order.
  • Group Discussions: Four Discernments that Ignatius Made
